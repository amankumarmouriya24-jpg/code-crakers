# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making this project welcoming, respectful, and useful for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Give feedback that helps improve the project.
- Respect different skill levels and backgrounds.
- Focus discussions on the work and its impact.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Disruptive or bad-faith participation

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project community.
